About Me

Hazel Kasusky is an artist manager actively involved in local music communities. She assists with audio engineering, producing, songwriting, and managing roles with artsits and other creatives. One of her main goals is to bridge together local businesses with local artists to promote self-sustaining economic models in Los Angeles neighborhoods.

When she isn't working on music, Hazel likes to try new recipes and play random Steam video games. She also enjoys driving around Los Angeles and discovering great photoshoot locations. Feel free to contact her about music ideas and collaborations. Or if you just want to talk about anime or books.
